Hoedown at Hog and Hominy

The Hoedown, hosted by Andy Ticer and Michael Hudman, will open the new Hog and Hominy Restaurant at 707 West Brookhaven Circle, Memphis,Tn.
on Sunday Afternoon, July 22, 4 til 8pm. The First Annual Hog and Hominy Bocce Tournament will follow at 8pm!

There'll be square dancing, fiddlin' by Memphis' finest, Donna Wolf, and the Bluff City Backsliders playin' on the porch! All this plus Fun Food, Drinks and a cool Silent Auction featuring some of Memphis' best artists.

Co-chairs Rena Chiozza, Pat McRee, and Richard Hudman invite you to a one-of-a-kind celebration benefitting the Flying Colors Cancer Network's new "Speak Up and Get Checked" oral cancer awareness initiative and providing assistance for those diagnosed.

Tickets are just $25!
Brookhaven Circle Bash

Event chair, Rena Chiozza is planning the Second Annual Brookhaven Bash September 30, 2012 from 1 til 5pm

This event showcases the diverse businesses on Brookhaven Circle and other Memphis vendors. Families will enjoy food by Brookhaven Restaurants, entertainment and there will be activities for children and an art show at Studio 708.

A portion of the proceeds will benefit the Flying Colors Cancer Network.
A Hair Raising Event

Hosted by chefs Andrew Ticer and Michael Hudman, the Fifth Annual Hair-Raising Event will be at Andrew Michael Italian Kitchen, 712 West Brookhaven Circle, Oct. 1, 5:00 til 9:00.

Co- chairs, Rena Chiozza and Pat McRee gather small art from the best of the Mid South artist community for a delightful silent auction, great entertainment, and wonderful food.

Proceeds help the Flying Colors Cancer Network purchase wigs for women on chemotherapy.
