About the Artist

Artist Statement: I awake each day to the gift of life and the unending beauty that surrounds me. I thrill to the challenges and surprises that develop in each painting. The privilege of sensing life and letting it flow through my fingers and onto canvas is joyful!

Judy Joy has had the good fortune to study with some of todayâ€™s finest contemporary masters such as Jeff Legg, Dawn Whitelaw, Roger Dale Brown, Tim Horn, and Qiang Huang to name a few. Her work has been juried into local and regional shows and she is represented by The Tennessee Artisan Gallery and Market in Dickson, Tennessee. Judy Joy can be found painting in her studio or en plein air everyday in her hometown of Germantown, Tennessee.

She is a member of the Oil Painters of America, American Women Artists, American Impressionist Society, The Chestnut Group, and The Tennessee Art League.
